Career Awareness (Class 8‚Äì10)

  • What is a career?
  • Why is choosing the right career important?
  • What are your interests and strengths?
  • What is the difference between a job and a career?
  • What happens after 10th class?
  • Why are English and Mathematics important?
  • Can skills be learned without high marks?
  • What is lifelong learning?

Career Awareness (Intermediate)

  • What are the career options after Intermediate?
  • What is Engineering and what do engineers do?
  • Difference between B.Tech, Degree, and Diploma
  • Are government jobs the only safe option?
  • What are new-age careers?
  • What mistakes students make while choosing careers?
  • How to choose a career based on interest?

Basic Technology Awareness

  • What is a computer program?
  • What is programming in simple words?
  • Why do we need programming languages?
  • Is coding only for engineers?
  • Where is programming used in daily life?
  • What is Python and where is it used?
  • What is a website?
  • Difference between website and mobile app

Modern Technologies & Future Skills

  • What is Artificial Intelligence (AI)?
  • How is AI used in daily life?
  • What is Cyber Security?
  • Why is data important?
  • What skills are important for the future?
  • Will technology create or remove jobs?
  • How can students prepare from school level?

Purpose: These questions encourage thinking, awareness, and confidence among students without academic pressure.

Career Guidance

  • Career options after 10th
  • Career options after Intermediate
  • Engineering, Medicine, Government Jobs & New-Age Careers
  • Importance of skills, mindset & continuous learning

Technology Awareness (Basic Level)

  • What is Programming?
  • What is Python used for?
  • What are Websites & Mobile Apps?
  • Introduction to AI, Cyber Security & Data Science

Note: This is awareness-level education, not syllabus replacement.
